Planter

Description

Planter can be used to measure if your plants are getting enough water and sunlight.

For most house plants it is recommeded that they get 2,000-10,000 LUX of sunlight for 12-14 hours per day. The 4 Digit display will display the number of hours and minutes of acceptable sunlight that your plant has gotten in a 24 hour time span.
The moisture sensor measures the soil for oversaturation and undersaturation. The LED Bar displays how close the soil is to 475 which is in the center of the range of values that represent humid soil. If it is oversaturated or undersaturated, the LED Bar will display red and if not it will show green and all values in between will be represented accordingly.

Step by Step(Pictures of Setup)

Step 1

Attach the Arduino Dock to the Base Shield

Step 2

Attach the 4 Digit Display to the Base Shield in D2

Step 3

Attach the Digital Light Sensor to the Base Shield in I2C

Step 4

Attach the LED Bar to the Base Shield in D4

Step 5

Attach the Moisture Sensor to the Base Shield in A0

Step 6

SSH into the arduino and set "omega2-ctrl gpiomux set i2c gpio"

Step 7

Run the program with the moisture sensor in the soil and the Digital light sensor in the plant.
